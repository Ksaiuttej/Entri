History of TCS:

Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) is a global IT services, consulting, and business solutions organization that was founded in 1968 by Tata Sons, the holding company of the Tata Group. The company is headquartered in Mumbai, India, and is currently one of the largest IT services companies in the world.
